Ingredients

  • Potatoes (especially good for this recipe "Yukon gold")
  • Olive oil
  • Seasoning: garlic, rosemary, paprika, chili powder and any other if you want!
    •

    How to cook?

    1. Scrub potatoes and slice them very thin even thinner than a coin
    2. Set medium or slightly high heat and add olive oil
    3. Add your potatoes and salt, pepper, rosemary
    4. Cook potatoes until all of them have golden crispy underneath after that flip them to the other side
    5. When crispy is done on the other side add all your other seasonings
    6. Sometimes flip potatoes until they are cooked inside (they are cooked when soft inside)

    This you should have when you are done
