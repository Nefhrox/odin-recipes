Ingredients

  • Bell pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Sesoning: salt, pepper

Lets get started!

  1. Take tomatoes rinse them with water, remove green cores from vegetables and cut tomatoes into small cubes
  2. Peel the onions rinse them with water and cut into small cubes
  3. Heat 20 ml of vegetable oil in a pan add the onions and fry for about 2 minutes on medium heat
  4. Add tomatoes and seasoning
  5. Simmer 5-7 minutes
  6. Rinse the peppers then fry them on each side for 3â€“4 minutes under a lid until the skin is blistered
  7. Then turn off the heat and remove the peppers from the pan
  8. Add tomatoes with onions to peppers and enjoy!
